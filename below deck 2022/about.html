About Us

Our Story

Sicne 2009 our members have been active in the yacht space and we have created the best value for money for all of our past clients! That is also the reason why all of our clients come back every summer and sail in the greek waters with our help.

Yachts

We only work with the most exclusive yachts, we prefer quality over quantity and that is why we have a limited number of yachts available for our clients. All of our yachts are new, privately owned, luxurious and have been maintained excellently.

Locations(Greece)

Below Deck is a Greek yacht charter company, based in Athens. We operate in the best locations, currently we charter yachts in the Saronic gulf , in the Ionian sea and the Aegean sea and we are expanding in more and more ports every year.

Our Vision

We are committed to expanding our network of offices, employing the best professionals in the industry and investing in the development of proprietary software solutions, to deliver our clients unrivaled service of the highest industry standard. We offer forward-thinking advice, insight and support to yacht owners, buyers, charterers and captains worldwide. Our focus is on leading the industry with a client-centric approach.
